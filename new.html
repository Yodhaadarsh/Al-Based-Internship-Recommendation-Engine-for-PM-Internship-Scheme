About PM Internship Yojna

PM Internship Yojna is a national-level internship initiative designed to connect students and fresh graduates with meaningful internships across government bodies, research institutions, and partner organizations. The program focuses on capacity building, mentorship and practical exposure to public administration and applied projects.
